Industry Data

Comparison of group job profiles with Burning Glass industry data

The ideal Job titles of each group member are listed in the table below. In order to compare these to the Burning Glass industry data we had to match them with existing job profiles that might differ slightly to the selected ones. The following adjustments have been made for the comparison:

Data Scientist -> Senior Data Engineer

Head of Digital Marketing & e-Commerce -> Customer Engagement Manager

Divisional IT Manager -> IT Operations Manager

Every job profile is divided into five general skills and five IT skills. Those skills are ranked according to the Top 25 General/IT Skills Burning Glass industry data. The lowest score achievable is 15 and means that the ideal job profile demands the Top 5 skills in a category. The highest score is 115 and means that the ideal job profile demands the 5 lowest scored skills in a category.

The average value in the category “General Skills” for all job profiles is 48, meaning that the skills demanded for each job are among the top skills demanded in the industry. The Divisional IT Manager skill set is the most demanded with a score of 36.

The average value in the category “IT Skills” for all job profiles is 45, meaning that the IT skills demanded for each job are also among the top skills demanded in the industry. Again, the Divisional IT Manager skills set is the most demanded with a score of 28.

The three highest ranked “General Skills” that are not mentioned in any of the group’s ideal jobs are Organisational Skills, Writing and Troubleshooting.

The three highest ranked “IT Skills” that can not be found in any of the group’s ideal jobs are Microsoft Windows, SAP and Building Relationships.

Matching the ideal job profiles of all group members with the Top IT Jobs reveals a rather low ranking for all positions.

However, the industry data counts job postings and as all ideal jobs are senior management positions the number of postings is expected to be much lower than other jobs like Java Developers, Test Analysts or Software Engineers. Therefore, for all group members the decision to pursue a career in the chosen job category has not changed after seeing Burning Glass industry data.
